####GEORGE BROWN HISTORY:

--George Brown College was founded in 1967 and named in honor of George Brown,
--one of Canada's Fathers of Confederation and a prominent 19th-century newspaper publisher.
--The college was established as a public, post-secondary institution dedicated to providing
--high-quality education and training to meet the evolving needs of students and the job market.

--Over the years, George Brown College has grown to become one of the largest and most respected
--colleges in Ontario, offering a wide range of programs in various fields such as health sciences,
--business, hospitality, technology, and the arts. It has multiple campuses across Toronto, each specializing in different disciplines.
